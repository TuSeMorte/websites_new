About

I am a web developer and runner. I started working professionally with computers in 1995. Starting with hardware development and moving quickly into software development. My journey progressed through the years, from local applications to network applications. Finally, it moved to web-based applications. Eventually, I went back to school to finish my bachelor's degree in Information Technology, specializing in web applications. I currently work with Microsoft's AZURE platform and various other cloud-based technologies. I enjoy doing online coding challenges and developing websites and apps that perform a specific purpose, some of which can be found here and on Github.

I also enjoy running. Trail running, road running, running alone, and running with friends and family. Running allows me personally to escape whatever is causing me stress or causing me troubles in my own life.
